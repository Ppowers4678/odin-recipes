Smores Recipe

Delicious Smores

This tasty treat is loaded with sugary goodness. Whether you're camping outside or on the couch with a cup of hot cocoa this is a go to all-american delicacy. There's never a bad time for smores.

Time to Cook: Est 2 minutes!!

Serves: As many as you'd like to make for!

Ingrediants

  • chocolate
  • graham crackers
  • marshmellows

Instructions

  1. Set graham crackers on plate
  2. Heat marshmellows with whatever preferred method
  3. Set marshmellows and chocolate on crackers
  4. Enjoy!!!
