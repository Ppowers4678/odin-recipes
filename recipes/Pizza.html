Pizza

This Homemade Cheese Pizza is delicious and easy to make.

Serves 2-3

Estimated Time: 1 Hour

Ingrediants

  • flour
  • yeast
  • pizza sauce
  • cheese
  • olive oil
  • butter

Cooking Instruction

  1. butter a pizza pan and preheat oven to 360 degrees
  2. combine flour with yeast and knead dough into a circle with the edge raised to form crust
  3. spread oil evenly over dough
  4. spread tomato sauce evenly over dough
  5. spread cheese evenly over dough
  6. bake for 25 minutes or until crust is golden brown
  7. enjoy!!!
